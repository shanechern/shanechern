Resources for "Multi-headed lattices and Green functions"

This webpage contains Mathematica resources for the paper "Multi-headed lattices and Green functions" by Shane Chern and Lin Jiu.

You may find the Mathematica notebooks and PDF screenshots (in case you have no access to Mathematica) for the results in this paper. Since some computations for creative telescoping certificates take too much time, we include these certificates in separate files if you do not want to execute such exhausting computations.
